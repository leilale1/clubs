Walpole High Clubs & Activities

An important part of the Walpole High School is becoming involved in our school's extensive extracurricular organizations and events. These activities are provided to give students comprehensive and beneficial social, athletic, and academic experiences. We have about 30 clubs and organizations! Some of our popular clubs include:

UNICEF

Youth-led initiative that partners with the U.S. Fund for UNICEF to educate, advocate, and fundraise to support UNICEF’s work in helping the world’s children survive. Students support the U.S. Fund for UNICEF through our annual Trick-or-Treat fundraiser, our annual Egg-My-Yard fundraiser, and smaller fundraisers.

Community Service Club

Group dedicated to making a positive impact within the community and school. Members help with school events and support local charities to organizing neighborhood cleanups and assist at community centers. Excellent opportunity for students to give back, make lasting connections, and create meaningful change in the world.

Speech and Debate

Compete with other actors or orators. Dig into real-world issues, conduct research, understand the perspectives of those with whom you do not agree, and cite sources. Members build skills that will be valuable throughout their academic and professional careers. You’ll get to know students from Walpole and many other high schools.

Drama

Open to any and all students at WHS who have a love for theatre, both on stage and off stage. Drama Club gives three productions every year. There are plenty of opportunities to get involved, from acting on stage, to participating in set, prop, costume, and technical design. No matter your interest, you can find a home on the Walpole High stage.

Robotics

Focuses on giving students the opportunity to work with industry professionals to design, build, wire, and program robots to play off against other high schools in New England and around the world. Each fall you can learn and build skills in CAD, programming, and machine shop use and safety while building simple mechanisms and upgrades to robots.

Tri-M Music Honor Society

Designed to recognize students for their academic and musical achievements, reward them for their accomplishments and service activities, and to inspire other students to excel at music and leadership. Offers a complete system of rewards that help inspire students and recognize excellence in individuals.
